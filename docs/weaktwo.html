Weak Two-Bids

All suit openings on the two level, except 2♣1The strong (20+ HCP) bid., are weak and natural. They show a preemptive holding, with a decent six-card suit, and insufficient points to open with a constructive (one-level) bid.

The quality of the opened suit varies with position and vulnerability – third-seat openings are the lightest, and second- and (especially) fourth-seat bids are more sound. Playing green against red, a third-seat 2♠ opening with only a jack2Sometimes even a good five-card suit. is possible.

Raises are only non-forcing bids. All other suit bids are natural and forcing for one round, and 2N is an artificial asking bid. A raise to game is two-way, made either with a strong hand that expects to make or furthering the preempt.

Ogust

The 2N response to a weak-two bid asks opener to describe his strength and suit quality.
